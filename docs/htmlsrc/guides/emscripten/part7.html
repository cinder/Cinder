Additional potentially helpful information

  1. It may be beneficial to run emcc --clear-cache now and then to ensure you get a clean build.

  2. Run export EMCC_DEBUG=1 before building to tell Emscripten to spit out it's own debugging information. You can optionally pass in -v as an Emscripten flag

  3. You can of course, use your own HTML shell for building your app. See here for details

  4. If you're curious about what other flags you can add when building your Emscripten project

  5. From time to time, you may notice that Emscripten has stopped compiling new changes, which can be a pain. You'll have to remember to make a small tweak in one of your main specified source files to get things to re-compile.

  6. For faster load times, assuming you don't need to examine the output, it may be a good idea to just do release builds.
